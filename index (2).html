Chest & Triceps Workout

Flat Barbell Bench Press

4 sets × 6-8 reps

Muscles: Pectoralis Major, Triceps, Front Deltoids

Tips: Keep feet planted, slight arch in back, bar to lower chest

Incline Dumbbell Press

3 sets × 8-10 reps

Muscles: Upper Pectorals, Front Deltoids

Tips: Set bench to 30-45° angle, palms facing forward

Dips (Weighted if possible)

3 sets × 8-10 reps

Muscles: Lower Pectorals, Triceps

Tips: Lean forward to emphasize chest, control descent

Exercise Demonstration

Flat Barbell Bench Press

  • Lie flat on bench with eyes under the bar
  • Grip bar slightly wider than shoulder-width
  • Keep feet flat on floor and butt on bench
  • Lower bar to lower chest with control
  • Press bar straight up without bouncing
